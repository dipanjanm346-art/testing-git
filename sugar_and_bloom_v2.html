Sugar & Bloom began as one oven and a stubborn belief that a cake should look like it was grown, not assembled. We press real petals into sugar, fold seasonal fruit through the crumb, and finish every tier by hand.

Nothing here is mass-made. You tell us the occasion; we bake the one cake for it — matched to the day, the flavours you love, and the table it will sit on. Then it leaves the counter still warm at heart.
